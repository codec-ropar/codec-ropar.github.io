Coding Club IITRPR

Coding Club is one of the most active clubs of the institute.
Spreading knowledge and growing the competitive coding culture is at the root of the existence of this club.

About Us

Thinking differently, scourging for corner cases and being greedy with time defines this community.

We organize coding competitions on various popular platforms to help students excel in competitive coding.

Frequent sessions are held where algorithms and data structures are put up for discussion and scrutiny, with the motivation to do something more and perform better with every competition.
